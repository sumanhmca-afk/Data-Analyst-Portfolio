# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Objective
To deliver a high-level executive dashboard summarizing overall sales performance, highlighting key revenue drivers, product performance, and seasonal trends.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI (Data Visualization, DAX)

## 📂 Dataset
The dataset consists of sales transactions including product categories, revenue, and time-based data.

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards (Total Revenue, Sales Overview)
- Category-wise Sales Analysis
- Product-level Performance Tracking
- Monthly Sales Trend Visualization

## 📈 Key Insights
- **Total Revenue:** Generated a total sales volume of **2.25M**.
- **Category Leader:** Technology category leads with **36.66% contribution ($825.86K)**.
- **Top Product:** Canon imageCLASS copier is the highest-selling product.
- **Seasonality:** Sales peak during **September and December**, with a noticeable drop in **February**.

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- **Inventory Optimization:** Maintain higher stock levels for Technology products and top-performing items.
- **Seasonal Strategy:** Launch targeted promotions during low-performing months (January–February).
- **Product Focus:** Prioritize high-demand products to maximize profitability.

## 📌 Conclusion
This dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly understand business performance and supports strategic decision-making through interactive visual insights.
