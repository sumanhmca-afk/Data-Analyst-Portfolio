# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Analysis

## 📌 Objective
To analyze e-commerce sales data and monitor key business metrics, focusing on category performance, monthly growth trends, and revenue distribution.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts)

## 📂 Dataset
The dataset includes transactional sales data such as product categories, sales values, and time-based information.

## 🔍 Analysis Performed
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Creation of pivot tables for category and time-based analysis
- Visualization of sales trends using charts

## 📈 Key Insights
- **Total Revenue:** Achieved a total sales value of **2.25M**, indicating strong overall performance.
- **Sales Distribution:** Furniture and Office Supplies contribute a balanced share, reducing dependency on a single category.
- **Seasonal Trends:** A significant spike in sales is observed in **November**, likely driven by seasonal demand.
- **Product Concentration:** A small set of top-performing products generates a large portion of total revenue.

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- **Seasonal Planning:** Increase marketing efforts in October to maximize November sales opportunities.
- **Category Expansion:** Invest in Office Supplies due to its stable and consistent demand.
- **Product Strategy:** Focus on high-performing products to maximize revenue growth.

## 📌 Conclusion
This analysis provides actionable insights into sales patterns and helps in making data-driven decisions for improving revenue and operational efficiency.
