# 📊 Customer Segmentation Analysis

## 📌 Objective
To segment customers based on income and spending behavior, enabling targeted marketing strategies and improved customer engagement.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts, Slicers)

## 📂 Dataset
The dataset includes customer demographic and behavioral data such as income, spending score, and gender.

## 🔍 Analysis Performed
- Data categorization based on income and spending patterns
- Creation of segments (High, Medium, Low-value customers)
- Visualization of customer distribution

## 📈 Key Insights
- **Demographics:** Male customers slightly outnumber female customers.
- **Value Segments:** Customers are fairly evenly distributed across High, Medium, and Low-value groups.
- **Revenue Contribution:** High-value customers generate the largest share of revenue despite smaller group size.
- **Growth Opportunity:** Medium-value customers show strong potential to convert into high-value customers.

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- **Loyalty Programs:** Introduce exclusive rewards for high-value customers to improve retention.
- **Upselling Strategy:** Target medium-value customers with personalized offers to increase spending.
- **Customer Engagement:** Develop tailored marketing campaigns based on customer segments.

## 📌 Conclusion
Customer segmentation provides valuable insights into customer behavior, helping businesses design targeted strategies to increase revenue and customer satisfaction.
